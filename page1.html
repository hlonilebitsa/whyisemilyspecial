Chapter One

To write this short story and show Emily why she was so special, Hloni had to do extensive research which mainly included our chats and whatever was out there. Well, I hope you’re ready, let’s go to it ;)

Hloni met Emily 22 February this year. Ever since he got close to her, he made it his main mission to really show her why she’s so special. What is it about her she can’t see about herself. Well, let’s start with her kind heart.

There is something rather special in the way that you bring your kindness, it does not come across loudly or obnoxiously but rather through the smallest acts that mean the world. It is in the way that she listens, listening for the sake of it, each and every word having meaning for her then breaking it down to me.

Her kindness is in the gentleness of her heart, caring without needing anything in return and giving people the sense of feeling special without trying. There is an innocence and serenity that comes with her, and it just makes things that much better around her.

The amazing part is that you do not even know how unique this all is because it comes natural to you, being who you are. In some cases, Hloni see how she even put others before herself. There are those who took advantage of it but there are those who took it well. That takes real courage and a very pure heart to do that.
