Final Chapter

Emily really is a truly extraordinary human being. It took Hloni weeks to begin understanding just how amazing and capable she truly is. Hearing how she experienced moments in life where she wasn’t always prioritized by those who were meant to love her deeply stayed with him.

How does someone so joyful end up experiencing such highs and lows? And then it became clear—no matter how much good you give to people, they can still choose to take it for granted. No one should ever have to experience the opposite of the greatness they give to others.

Hloni realised he likes Emily for many reasons, but her soul is the one that stands above everything. The most beautiful thing about you, Emily, is that you don’t remind me of anyone. Everything about you feels new, unrepeatable.

You’re gorgeous, but that is honestly the least interesting thing about you. Yes, you are beautiful—anyone with eyes can see that. You walk into a room and people notice you without even trying. But beauty is the easiest thing to see; it takes no effort to point out a face.

What I see in you takes attention. It takes time. It takes knowing you slowly and understanding you deeply. That is what makes you unforgettable. I notice the way your voice changes during calls when you talk about something you truly care about—not just the spark, but the focus, the passion. It’s like watching a flame catch, even when you try to hide it.

Maybe you don’t realise it, but that passion is one of the most beautiful things about you. You once told me to stop being afraid to let the inner boy out. That made me realise something powerful: you will never feel ready, because being ready is not a feeling—it’s a decision.

I don’t ever want Emily to feel like a second choice to anyone. I don’t want her to ever feel like she is number two. She has given so much in this life that she deserves to know what it feels like to be chosen first—consistently, intentionally, and fully.

I may not have always experienced that myself, but that never stops me from trying to be the best version of myself for you. I want to treat you in a way that shows the people who never valued you what they missed. I want you to see how happy you can be when you are chosen properly.

I want to be gentle with you. I want to show you what it means for someone to consistently pick you. YOU ARE ENOUGH!
