Chapter Three

There’s something deeply special about the way she shows support. It’s not just in the big moments, but in the quiet. You take the time to understand the things Hloni likes, even the little details that most people would overlook, and somehow, she makes them feel important too.

It’s in the way she listens with genuine curiosity, the way her eyes light up when he talks about something that matters to him, as if his passions become hers for a moment. She doesn’t just stand beside him, she walks with him, learning, asking, caring.

And in doing that, you create this beautiful sense of connection. Her support isn’t just something you give…it’s something you feel, deeply and effortlessly, every single time he talks to her.

She showed support to the fullest when Hloni came clean about his health and family past. She took moments to actually want to come and see him when he was still in hospital.
