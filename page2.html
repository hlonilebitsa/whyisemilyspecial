Chapter Two - Hopeful

Her hopeful character is so powerful that it never goes unseen, yet it is present in your perspective. Although not everything is rosy at times, she is capable of finding a way to see the future as positive and something that will eventually happen on its own.

Her hope is reflected in your ability to smile despite your doubts and fears, and your eyes that sparkle despite anything else. You make others feel that the next day is going to be better than the previous one, that something wonderful awaits them, despite being invisible.

She fed Hloni that hope even through times where things seemed impossible. She accepted Hloni the way he was and never found reason to turn her back on him regardless of the situation. She embodies what it means to be hopeful about the road you’re walking on, and that it’s always going to be okay.

She truly represents what it means to smile no matter how bad the road is. She reminds Hloni of All Might from My Hero Academia (anime he recently finished). He tackles all the crime in the city with a smile on his face no matter how bad the situation looks and says, “Don’t worry, I am here!”
